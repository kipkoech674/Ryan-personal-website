Technology & Creative Interests

During my high school years, I developed a strong interest in computers and digital creativity. I enjoyed working with design tools and exploring how technology could be used to communicate ideas visually.

  • Designed posters for school events and activities
  • Created simple digital graphics and layouts
  • Applied creativity and attention to detail in visual designs
  • Developed basic knowledge of color theory, typography, and layout design
  • Used computer software to enhance presentation and communication

These early experiences strengthened my creativity, problem-solving skills, and interest in technology, which later influenced my decision to pursue Software Engineering at university.

Extracurricular Activities

In addition to academics, I actively participated in sports during my secondary school education. I was a member of my school’s football team, where I played as a left back.

  • Played as a left back for my secondary school football team
  • Participated in regular training sessions and inter-school competitions
  • Developed teamwork, discipline, leadership, and strategic thinking

Balancing academics and sports helped me build time management skills, resilience, and a strong sense of responsibility, which continue to support my academic and personal growth.
